<h3>Privacy statement</h3>
<h4>Singpass Privacy Statement</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>This Singpass Privacy Statement (the <strong>"Privacy Statement"</strong>)
must be read in conjunction with the Singpass Terms of Use (the <strong>"Terms"</strong>).
Unless otherwise stated, all other capitalised terms contained in this
Privacy Statement shall have the meaning given to it in the Terms. In this
Privacy Statement, "we", "us" or "our" refers collectively to the Government
of Singapore and Government Technology Agency.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The purpose of this Privacy Statement is to inform you of how we collect,
use, disclose and process the following data and information:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>data and personal data relating to you;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>information associated with your Account (including but not limited to
Singpass, Singpass app or Account activity or interaction data); and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>data and personal data that may relate to a third party individual and
is provided by you</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>(collectively, <strong>"Data"</strong>).</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Types of Data that we collect</u>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We may request certain types of Data from you in connection with your
Use of Singpass and Singpass Materials. Examples of the types of Data which
we may request and collect include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>information relating to your Account, such as your Account number;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>information about you from data source(s) approved by us;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>your contact information, including your telephone number, email and mailing
addresses;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>information from your Device including but not limited to your location
information, IP address, MAC address, network interfaces, device type and
model, and other forms of data from your Device in connection with the
Use of Singpass or Singpass Materials;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>information relating to the version of the Singpass app on your Device
or your Use of Singpass or Singpass Materials, including but not limited
to information relating to your activities in respect of Singpass and Singpass
Materials, the usage of the various functionalities, features or clicks
on the Singpass Materials, Device permissions such as the permission to
push notifications, use location services, use any connectivity services,
and other similar information;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fingerprint, Face Identification enrolled by, where applicable, the relevant
Fingerprint Recognition Feature or Face Recognition Feature on your Registered
Mobile Device;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>your Captured Face Data;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>your Login Credentials for Using Singpass and Singpass Materials; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>such other information as may be provided by, or collected from you in
connection with your Use of Singpass and Singpass Materials.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>We may collect the Data through various technologies including but not
limited to:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><u>Analytics Tags</u>: Analytics Tags are used by us to analyse the effectiveness
of the functionalities and features of Singpass and Singpass Materials
and user preferences relating to Singpass and Singpass Materials in order
to improve general user experience. We may use information collected through
such analytics tags in combination with your Data (such as transaction
history and demographic information) to understand more about the profile
of our users.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Cookies</u>: Cookies are small data files which are sent to your browser
or Device to store and track information about you when you Use Singpass
and Singpass Materials. Cookies are also used to improve and maintain the
functionality of Singpass and Singpass Materials, and to track information
such as the number of users of Singpass and Singpass Materials, their frequency
of use, the profile of the users and other similar information relating
to the Use of Singpass and Singpass Materials. While these cookies can
tell us when you Use Singpass and Singpass Materials, they cannot read
data off your hard disk. Not all cookies collect personal data and you
may configure your preferences and settings to reject the use of cookies.
However, this may mean you may not be able to take full advantage of Singpass
and Singpass Materials, including the features or the functionalities of
Singpass or Singpass Materials.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Server Logs</u>: Server logs are files and records of activity created
by the Device that you Use Singpass or Singpass Materials from. For example,
a web server log may record a history of page requests whereas other logs
may record errors, access attempts or other information about your Device,
such as your IP address or your Device's cookie settings. We collect and
retain such server logs for administrative, security and troubleshooting
purposes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Device information</u>: Information relating to your Device such as
its model, type, operating system and other technical attributes.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Why we collect Data</u>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Data collected from you may be used, disclosed and processed by us for
any, some or all of the following purposes:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>to assist, process and facilitate your Use of Singpass and Singpass Materials,
including registering and creating your Account, facilitating your login
into your Account and your use of any features or functionalities of Singpass
and Singpass Materials (including Singpass Face Verification and Myinfo);</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to carry out your instructions or respond to any queries, feedback or
complaints provided by (or purported to be provided by) you or on your
behalf, or otherwise for the purposes of responding to or dealing with
your interactions with us;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to monitor and track your usage of Singpass and Singpass Materials, in
order to assist us in understanding user behaviour, trends and preferences,
and in improving Singpass and Singpass Materials. For the avoidance of
doubt, we may also collect, use, disclose and process such Data to create
reports and produce statistics regarding the above for record-keeping and
reporting or publication purposes (whether internally or externally). Data
used in such reports and statistics will be anonymised before publication;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>for the purposes of storing or creating backups of your Data for contingency
or business continuity purposes;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to prevent or investigate any fraud, unlawful or criminal activity or
omission or misconduct, or cybersecurity incident, via the use of your
Account, whether or not there is any suspicion of the aforementioned, even
if the disclosure of such Data may be potentially incriminating;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to deal with conflict of interests or investigate complaints in relation
to your Account;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to authenticate your identity through the use of Singpass Face Verification;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to populate your Profile Data for the use of Myinfo;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to pre-fill any e-form, user registration page or screen on any Approved
Recipient's Channel(s) with your Profile Data;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to contact you or communicate with you on any matters relating to your
Use of Singpass and Singpass Materials, including but not limited to the
purposes set out above, via email, push notifications or such other forms
of communication that we may introduce from time to time depending on the
functionality of Singpass and Singpass Materials and your Device;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>for us or other Public Agencies to contact you or communicate with you
for purposes which include facilitating the discharge of public functions
by us or such Public Agencies, and the conduct of market research, surveys
and promotional activities by Public Agencies; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>for any other purpose(s) that do not appear above where permissible under
any written law</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>(collectively, the <strong>"Purposes"</strong>).</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>With regard to Data provided by you or which we have collected as part
of the provision of Singpass or its features or functionalities:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>unless prohibited by written law, we may share, transfer and disclose
Data with any of the following third parties:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>third parties approved by us to use Singpass and its features or functionalities
(such as Approved Service Providers);</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>relevant third party service providers in connection with the provision
of Singpass and Singpass Materials;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>law enforcement authorities, investigative bodies, regulatory bodies;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Public Agencies; and</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>for your convenience, we may also display to you Data you had previously
supplied us. This will speed up the transaction(s) and save you the trouble
of repeating previous submissions. Should such displayed Data be out-of-date,
you shall be required to supply the latest Data through the relevant functionality
in Singpass without delay.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Data of third party individuals</u>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the course of your Use of Singpass or Singpass Materials, you may provide
or we may collect Data relating to other individuals from you.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You shall, prior to disclosing to us such Data relating to other individuals:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>notify these individuals (i) that you will be providing their data or
personal data to us or through your Use of Singpass or its features or
functionalities; and (ii) of the Purposes for which we will be collecting,
using, disclosing and processing their data or personal data, as set out
in this Privacy Statement and as notified by us to you from time to time;
and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>obtain the consent from the individuals whose data or personal data are
being disclosed, permitting (i) you to disclose the personal data to us
or through your Use of Singpass or its features or functionalities; and
(ii) us to collect, use, disclose and process these individuals' personal
data, for the Purposes.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>You represent and warrant that:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>the data or personal data that you will be providing us or have provided
us is, complete, accurate, true and correct; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>you have the valid authority and consent from these individuals to act
on their behalf and provide their data or personal data to us to collect,
use, disclose and process for the Purposes.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>How we handle and manage your Data</u>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Your Data may be stored in servers, systems or devices owned, operated
or managed by us, a relevant Third Party or any third party service provider
engaged by us or a relevant Third Party, or on your Device, and may be
used by us (or our third party service providers) and Approved Service
Providers to facilitate your Use of Singpass or Singpass Materials.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may withdraw your consent to the use and disclosure of Data by us
with reasonable notice; however, doing so may prevent proper Use of Singpass
or Singpass Materials, or any part thereof and may also result in the cessation
of your Use of Singpass or Singpass Materials, or any part thereof. We
may retain, use, disclose and process your Data for any of the Purposes
set out herein where permitted under any applicable law.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Changes to Privacy Statement</u>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We reserve the right to change, modify or supplement the Privacy Statement
at our discretion and at any time, by posting the changed, modified or
supplemented Privacy Statement on or through the Singpass Website, Singpass
app, or through such other means as we may deem appropriate. Your continued
Use of Singpass and Singpass Materials following the posting of any change,
modification or supplement will constitute your acceptance of such change,
modification or supplement. If you do not agree to any change, modification
or supplement to the Privacy Statement, please do not Use Singpass or Singpass
Materials.</p>
</li>
</ol>
